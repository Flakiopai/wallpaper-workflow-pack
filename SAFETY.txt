LOCAL AI WALLPAPER & PHOTO WORKFLOW
Safety, Ethics & Sovereign Rules

------------------------------------------------------------
CORE PRINCIPLE
------------------------------------------------------------

Everything in this workflow runs locally, on your hardware, under your
control. No cloud. No uploads. No telemetry. Your creative process stays
private and sovereign.


------------------------------------------------------------
RESPONSIBLE IMAGE GENERATION
------------------------------------------------------------

  • Keep everything offline and local
  • Only generate abstract or non-human content
  • Avoid anything that could resemble real people
  • Keep outputs clean, safe, and non-harmful


------------------------------------------------------------
RESPECTING CREATORS
------------------------------------------------------------

  • Do not mimic specific artists or copyrighted styles
  • Avoid prompts referencing real brands or IP
  • Keep everything original and abstract


------------------------------------------------------------
AVOIDING COPYRIGHTED CONTENT
------------------------------------------------------------

  • No characters, logos, or recognizable designs
  • No recreations of existing artwork
  • No references to trademarked names or franchises


------------------------------------------------------------
AVOIDING REAL PEOPLE
------------------------------------------------------------

  • No celebrity likenesses
  • No real faces
  • No "photo of a person" prompts — period


------------------------------------------------------------
KEEPING EVERYTHING OFFLINE
------------------------------------------------------------

  • All generation happens locally on your machine
  • No cloud services for generation, upscaling, or storage
  • No uploads to external servers
  • No telemetry or tracking tools in the workflow


------------------------------------------------------------
PRIVACY RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

  • No personal data in prompts
  • No identifiable features or user-specific content
  • Everything stays on your machine
  • No sharing of prompts or outputs to third-party services


------------------------------------------------------------
HARDWARE SAFETY
------------------------------------------------------------

  • Monitor system temps during long generation sessions
  • Close unnecessary apps to free VRAM and reduce thermal load
  • Don't run sustained heavy batches if your hardware is overheating
  • Give your machine breaks between large upscale batches


------------------------------------------------------------
ETHICAL GUARDRAILS
------------------------------------------------------------

  • Keep the workflow transparent and honest
  • Give credit to tools used without implying endorsement
  • Encourage users to choose their own preferred local software
  • Promote sovereign, local-first creative workflows
  • This pack teaches principles — not brand lock-in


------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT THIS PACK WILL NEVER ASK YOU TO DO
------------------------------------------------------------

  • Upload images or prompts to a cloud service
  • Generate content involving real people or likenesses
  • Copy copyrighted characters, brands, or artist styles
  • Share personal or identifiable information in prompts
  • Depend on a subscription or external account to function

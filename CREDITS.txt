LOCAL DIFFUSION GENERATOR
  Tool:    DiffusionBee
  Type:    Local, offline Stable Diffusion app
  Use:     Image generation at 1024 × 1024
  Note:    I personally used DiffusionBee to create the wallpapers in
           this pack. Users may substitute any local diffusion tool.

LOCAL UPSCALER
  Tool:    Upscayl
  Type:    Open-source, offline image upscaler
  Use:     4× upscale to 4096 × 4096
  Note:    I personally used Upscayl with the High Fidelity model.
           Users may substitute any local upscaling tool.

OPTIONAL SUPPORT TOOLS (REFERENCED, NOT INCLUDED)
  • ImageOptim — local image compression
  • Squoosh (offline mode) — local image compression
  • macOS Preview — cropping and resizing
  • GIMP / Photopea (offline mode) — optional color grading


------------------------------------------------------------
MODELS REFERENCED
------------------------------------------------------------

  • Stable Diffusion (default model bundled with DiffusionBee — version
    varies: SD 1.5, SD 2.1, or SDXL depending on app version)
  • High Fidelity / ESRGAN-based model (bundled with Upscayl)
